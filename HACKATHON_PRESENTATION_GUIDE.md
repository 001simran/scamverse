## 🎥 PRESENTATION TIPS

### During Presentation:
1. **Speak clearly and slowly** - judges will be taking notes
2. **Point at screen** - direct attention to key features
3. **Use dramatic pauses** - "Did you notice...?"
4. **Show emotion** - "This matters. People's lives are affected."
5. **Match narration to gameplay** - no talking during animations
6. **Be ready to pause/explain** - judges might ask mid-demo

### Body Language:
- Stand to the side (don't block screen)
- Make eye contact with judges
- Use hand gestures (but not excessive)
- Smile when appropriate (show passion!)

### If Something Goes Wrong:
- **Keep calm** - judges respect composure
- **Have a backup screenshot** - if game crashes
- **Pivot to talking points** - "While that loads, let me tell you..."
- **Show passion, not panic** - "This is why we need beta testing!"
